Welcome to Crack Detection Tracking System

Crack Detection Tracking in trains helps in identifying cracks and malfunctions on railway tracks, ensuring the safety and efficiency of rail operations. This system uses advanced technology to detect issues in real-time, allowing timely maintenance and reducing the risk of accidents.

Advantages:

  • Improved safety for trains and passengers.
  • Real-time monitoring of track conditions.
  • Efficient maintenance planning.
  • Cost reduction in long-term infrastructure management.
